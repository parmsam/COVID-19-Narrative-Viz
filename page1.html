COVID-19 Cases and Deaths in Marion County, Indiana for 2020 (as of June 24, 2020)

Marion County, Indiana holds the city of Indianapolis and represents the most populated county in Indiana with a population approaching one million (964,582 as of 2019). The first COVID-19 cases was detected on March 6, 2020, with the first COVID-19 death occuring on March 16. COVID-19 case diagnoses peaked in Marion County on Tuesday, May 5th at 305 cases with deaths peaking most recently on Wednesday, May 20 at 16 deaths.
